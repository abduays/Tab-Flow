🌊 TabFlow - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

TabFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles data and ensures your information remains secure.

2. Data Collection & Storage

Local-Only Processing: TabFlow operates entirely within your browser. All data including:

  • Tab activity timestamps
  • Usage history and patterns
  • Productivity analytics
  • Whitelist/blacklist settings

...is stored locally in your browser's storage (chrome.storage.local) and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Permissions Explained

TabFlow requests the following permissions for specific purposes:

  • tabs - To monitor tab activity and suspend inactive tabs
  • storage - To save your settings and analytics locally
  • alarms - To schedule periodic tab checks
  • activeTab - To identify the currently active tab
  • favicon - To display tab icons in the interface

4. What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No browsing history is sent to us
  • ❌ No personal information is collected
  • ❌ No tracking pixels or analytics services
  • ❌ No third-party data sharing
  • ❌ No account creation required

5. Third-Party Services

TabFlow does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The extension functions completely independently.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain complete control. Uninstalling the extension will remove all associated local data automatically.

7. Children's Privacy

TabFlow does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. Our extension doesn't collect personal information from anyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's privacy policy page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@tabflow.extension

By using TabFlow, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
